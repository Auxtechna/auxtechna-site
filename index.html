Who We Are

Auxtechna is a UK healthcare technology company working with NHS pharmacy and clinical teams to digitise the processes that still run on paper. Our focus is on software clinicians actually want to use: fast, clear, and built around the way NHS teams really work.

NHS-First Design

Every feature is shaped by NHS governance, procurement frameworks and the reality of how pharmacy and clinical teams work day to day. This isn't generic SaaS with a healthcare skin. It was written for the NHS from the start.

Security & Compliance

Each trust runs on its own isolated database. Prescriptions and PGDs are protected by Advanced Electronic Signatures with SHA-256 hashing. Every action is audited. Role-based access, Azure AD and NHS CIS2 single sign-on are all supported out of the box.

Built With Our Users

We ship updates regularly and take feature requests directly from the pharmacy teams, nurses and governance leads who use our software every day. If something isn't working for you, we want to hear about it and fix it.

Hosted For You

Everything runs in the cloud, so there's no on-premise kit to buy, no servers for your IT team to maintain, and no capital outlay. We handle hosting, updates, backups and support. Your team can focus on patient care.

Our Products

Two platforms tackling real, unresolved problems inside NHS pharmacy and governance teams.

Homecare Prescribing & Management

A digital platform covering the whole NHS homecare prescribing journey, from consultant clinic to the patient's front door. Prescribers, pharmacy teams, homecare suppliers and patients all work from the same record, with a full audit trail end to end.

  • Electronic prescribing with AES signatures
  • Real-time supplier portal integration
  • Patient self-service portal
  • Allergy checking & clinical safety
  • Multi-site trust support
  • NHMC & CQC-ready reporting
  • Repeat prescription management
  • Ward stock ordering module
Visit homecareconnects.co.uk
PGD Governance & Compliance

A governance platform built specifically for Patient Group Directions. It replaces the usual mix of Word documents, paper signatures and tracker spreadsheets with one system that's ready for CQC inspection.

  • Structured PGD creation with 10-step wizard
  • SHA-256 tamper-proof digital signatures
  • Staff authorisation & competency tracking
  • National NICE template adoption
  • Administration recording & adverse reactions
  • CQC evidence pack generation
  • Family versioning & superseding
  • NICE MPG2 framework aligned
Visit patientgroupdirection.co.uk
